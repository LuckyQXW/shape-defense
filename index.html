Shape Defense

The Shape Army is invading! Let's work together to defend our stronghold!

How to Play

The Magic Drawboard

Draw the shape for the ammo to be loaded next on the drawboard. Only ammo of the correct shape can eliminate the incoming enemies!

After drawing, press SPACE to load ammo, or CLEAR to clear the drawboard.

Playing with Arduino controllers

Use Joystick to select a cannon.

The small OLED screen shows the next wave!

Playing with keyboard

Use left and right arrow keys to select a cannon.
